A Shortcut to Mushrooms

Apr 19th 2022 pages 86-98

Chapter was pretty short, mostly describing the Hobbit's journey to the outskirts of the Shire

The trio of hobbits take a shortcut to Buckleberry, Frodo's new residence. The shortcut is off the beaten patha nd difficult to navigate compared to the road they were using but they would save time and possibly avoid the black riders that seem prowling about looking for Frodo.

They spot a black rider in the distance, but they hide amongst the trees and bristles. It would be difficult to get a horse to their location because of the the thorns and bog-like landscape they are traversing.

They reach an old farmer's place. It is Old Farmer Maggot, who's mushroom farm Frodo used to raid and get chased by Old Maggot's 3 dogs. Good thing Pippin is on friendly terms with the farmer.

The farmer says that and black rider came to his place earlier that evening asking about the whereabouts of 'Baggins' but Ol' Maggot ain't no snitch.

He takes the trio of hobbits to the ferry with his pony drawn wagon. It is dark and very foggy.

They hear the clip clop of horse hooves coming closer, closer.

A mysterious rider approaches.

Who is it but

Merry!
